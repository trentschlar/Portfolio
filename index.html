About Elizabeth Rentschlar

Elizabeth Rentschlar was born in Mt. Holly New Jersey, but her parents decided to move her to Texas as a child. She has now decided as an adult that this is a good time to move back to the state of her birth.

Elizabeth enjoys reading escapist novels that range in topics from sci-fi and fantasy to rom-coms and historical fiction. Her interest in experiences different from her own led her to be interested in geography as an undergraduate student.
